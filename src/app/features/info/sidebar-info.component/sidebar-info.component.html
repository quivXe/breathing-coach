Breathing Coach

Controlled breathing can reduce stress, improve focus, and relax your mind and body.

How It Works

  • Inhale: Breathe in slowly through your nose
  • Hold: Pause and let oxygen circulate
  • Exhale: Release gently through your mouth

Popular Patterns

Pattern Inhale Hold Exhale
Focus 4s 4s 8s
Relaxation & Sleep Aid 4s 7s 8s
Stress Relief 4s 4s 4s

Tips

  • Listen to your body — don’t overstrain
  • Adjust the durations to your comfort level
  • Consistency is more important than exact timing
  • Practice daily for maximum benefit
